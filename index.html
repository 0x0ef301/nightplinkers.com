Documented Risk Areas in Existing Airgun Literature

The following gap categories are routinely identified during documentation reviews. Unaddressed, each area represents a liability exposure vector for manufacturers and importers.

Risk Area Common Documentation Gap Liability Exposure
Torque Specifications Mounting hardware torque values absent or expressed in non-standard units Scope mount failure; accessory detachment under recoil
Degassing Procedures Ambiguous or absent step-by-step depressurization instructions for PCP and CO₂ platforms Uncontrolled pressure release; injury during disassembly or transport
Obstruction Protocol Barrel obstruction detection and clearance procedure gaps; no pre-fire inspection checklist Projectile-behind-obstruction discharge; barrel damage or personnel injury
Regulatory & Labeling Consistency Packaging, labeling, and manual safety language inconsistent across import chain Consumer misuse due to conflicting instructions; importer non-compliance
