Consulting for Manufacturers & Importers

Outdated manuals, ambiguous degassing procedures, and missing torque specifications create measurable liability exposure. Night Plinkers helps manufacturers, importers, and distributors close those gaps with field-informed documentation and structured safety standards.

Common documentation gaps that create liability exposure:
Risk Area Common Documentation Gap Liability Exposure
Torque Specifications Mounting hardware torque values absent or expressed in non-standard units Scope mount failure; accessory detachment under recoil
Degassing Procedures Ambiguous or absent step-by-step depressurization for PCP and CO2 platforms Uncontrolled pressure release; injury during disassembly or transport
Obstruction Protocol Barrel obstruction detection and clearance gaps; no pre-fire inspection checklist Projectile-behind-obstruction discharge; barrel damage or personnel injury
Regulatory & Labeling Consistency Packaging, labeling, and manual safety language inconsistent across import chain Consumer misuse due to conflicting instructions; importer non-compliance
